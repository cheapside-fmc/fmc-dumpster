Documents and Tools

Cheapside's FMC Resources (opens in new tab)

Provides various resources to use while making FMC Routes (for now, only the Aviation Information Publications needed to find real-life departure and arrival procedures)

FMC Route Converters (opens in new tab)

Converts other waypoint formats (such as those used with SkyVector and iFlightPlanner) into FMC routes, and further to other formats (such as Map Grid and a version compatible with Google My Maps)

Mr. Stoffer's FPDB converter (opens in new tab)

Converts from FPDB's .json format to FMC format
